Latest Earnings / Delivery Update

IonQ reported Q4 and full-year 2025 results (press/IR, Feb 2026): Q4 revenue ~$61.9M (~55% above the prior guidance midpoint; ~429% YoY), FY25 GAAP revenue $130.0M (~202% YoY; ~20% above prior full-year guidance). Management guided 2026 revenue to $225–$245M (midpoint ~$235M, ~81% YoY at midpoint) and highlighted RPO ~$370M at year-end alongside continued commercial traction. Near-term delivery focus: Q1'26 guide $48–$51M, scaling systems revenue, and integrating recent acquisitions while navigating GAAP volatility from warrant/fair-value accounting.

Bottom Line

IonQ is one of the clearest revenue growth stories in public quantum computing: prints are beating guidance, the 2026 outlook is aggressive but directionally consistent with RPO, and the balance sheet is fortress-like for a pre-profit platform. The catch is classic high-beta growth equity: GAAP earnings remain messy, the stock can re-rate both ways on sparse operating datapoints, and the Street’s upside case embeds sustained flawless execution. With composite risk at 59/100, we skew Hold / cautious accumulate for long horizons — size smaller, prefer staggered entries, and pair with strict risk budgets.
