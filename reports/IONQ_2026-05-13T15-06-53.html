Verdict · Speculative Buy / Trim Strength
IonQ delivers the best top-line growth in the public quantum cohort and a fortress balance sheet, but trades at a multiple that already prices in flawless execution through ~2030. Composite risk of 46/100 (Elevated) reflects extreme valuation and dilution offsetting a near-perfect growth scorecard. Existing holders: ride RPO conversion, but trim into strength above $70. New entries: prefer $35–45 retracement zones or post-print pullbacks. Avoid sizing as a core position; treat as a thematic call option on fault-tolerant quantum.
