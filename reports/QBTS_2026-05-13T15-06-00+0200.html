Bottom Line

D-Wave is the only commercial dual-platform quantum pure-play with a record $33.4M bookings quarter, $588M of cash, and a credible (if long-dated) gate-model roadmap via the Quantum Circuits acquisition. Counterweights are real and structural: Q1'26 GAAP revenue of just $2.9M against a $54.7M operating loss, a ~$130M annualized burn, persistent dilution, and warrant-driven GAAP volatility that masks underlying economics. With composite risk at 63/100, this is a speculative, high-beta thematic — appropriate for risk-budget growth/spec sleeves, inappropriate as a core position. We skew Hold with a tactical lean: position-size small, treat the Jun 1 Investor Day as a binary near-term re-rating event, and avoid chasing into momentum spikes given the >4× 52-week range.
